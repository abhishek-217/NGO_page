About Us

Lighting the Path to Change with NGO for Children Foundation

At NGO for Children, we are more than just an organization – we are a movement, driven by the belief that education is the cornerstone of empowerment. Established under the Indian Societies Act of 1860, we stand as a beacon of hope in underserved communities, dedicated to breaking the chains of poverty through the transformative power of learning.

Join us in our quest to rewrite the narrative of education, one success story at a time. Together, let’s build a world where every child has the opportunity to dream, learn, and soar. Welcome to NGO for Children Foundation – where hope is our currency, and education is our legacy.
